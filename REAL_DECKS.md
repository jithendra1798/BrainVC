# Real Pitch Decks — Provenance

Four famous, publicly released seed decks, transcribed faithfully into
ingestable markdown (`backend/seeds/decks/real_*.md`). The claims are the real
claims those founders made at the time; each deck was made public by the
founders themselves or their investors. On demo day: "we ran Airbnb's actual
2008 seed deck through the pipeline — here is the memo."

| Deck | File | Round | What really happened |
|---|---|---|---|
| AirBed&Breakfast (2008) | `real_airbnb_2008.md` | Raised ~$600K (Sequoia, Y Ventures) | Airbnb — $80B+ public co |
| Buffer (2011) | `real_buffer_2011.md` | Raised ~$450-500K from 18 angels | Profitable, $20M+ ARR |
| Coinbase (2012) | `real_coinbase_2012.md` | Raised $600K-1M seed (YC S12) | Public co, $50B+ at peak |
| UberCab (2008) | `real_ubercab_2008.md` | Raised ~$200K angel | Uber — $80B+ public co |

## Why this is a strong test

1. **The validator meets history**: Tavily checks 2008-2012 claims against
   today's web. Famous deck numbers (Craigslist 17K listings/week, $2M/day
   bitcoin volume) are widely documented — watch which claims get SUPPORTED
   by historical record vs. stay UNVERIFIABLE.
2. **The thesis lens is visible**: under the default AI-enterprise thesis these
   consumer/marketplace/crypto decks score off-thesis — switch to the
   `classic_seed` preset (below) and re-run to watch the same deck re-rank.
3. **Hindsight calibration**: all four became giant outcomes from decks full of
   unverifiable claims — the honest system should escalate, not pass. That IS
   the pre-seed problem, stated by the machine.
