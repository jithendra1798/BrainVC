Why It's Trustworthy

Trust is architecture, not vibes.

  • Separation of powers: the extractor cannot bless its own output — claims are born skeptical; only the validator (a different model) can raise trust.
  • Hallucination-proof citations by construction: models cite evidence by index; UUIDs are substituted in code; uncited claims are dropped.
  • Deterministic guardrail: a contradicted revenue claim makes INVEST structurally impossible — the LLM cannot override it.
  • Honest uncertainty: every score carries a confidence band whose basis is displayed; gaps are declared ("Cap table: not disclosed"), never fabricated.

Research · Area of Research 3, Actually Attempted

We tried to falsify our own scorer — and published the null.

  • Time-machine backtest: 5 eventual founders (HashiCorp, Vercel, Supabase, Deno, Astral) vs 5 elite non-founder controls — scored only on evidence created before each founding date, names blinded, exact permutation test (all 252 label assignments).
  • Finding — a robust null: AUC 0.20, p = 0.96. Elite builder capability does not predict who founds. At the elite tail, founding is a choice, not a capability threshold.
  • Hindsight, quantified: adding current-day star counts inflates every score (~+5; Rauch +10 from socket.io fame) without changing the ranking. Pipelines that score founders on today's footprint inherit this invisibly.
  • Consequence, already in the architecture: the cold-start score is a capability screen — one input into the Founder axis, never the axis itself. A sourcing system must not become a GitHub-stars fund.

Proof It Works

We ran Airbnb's actual 2008 seed deck through it.

  • Four real, founder-published seed decks ingested: Airbnb '08, Buffer '11, Coinbase '12, UberCab '08 — transcribed faithfully, sources on file.
  • The system escalates them: decks full of unverifiable self-reports should not auto-invest. That is the pre-seed problem, stated by a machine.
  • The scam deck (guaranteed 34% monthly yields, anonymous team) died at the gate in 2 seconds. Off-thesis decks get full analysis and an explained PASS — never a silent drop.
  • We hit — and fixed — the exact historical failure mode live: a gate that rejects on sector alone is how funds missed Uber. Ours is now calibrated and instructed against it.
