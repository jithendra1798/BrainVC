# GreenGrid AI — Forecasting for Distributed Energy

Load and generation forecasting for utilities managing rooftop solar and EV charging. Seed. Denver, US.

---

## The Problem

Distribution utilities fly blind on feeder-level load as solar and EVs proliferate.
Bad forecasts mean expensive peaker contracts and transformer failures.

---

## The Product

GreenGrid fuses smart-meter, weather, and EV-charging data into feeder-level
probabilistic forecasts. Grid planners get 48-hour ahead alerts on overload risk.

---
