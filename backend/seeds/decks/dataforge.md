# DataForge — Self-Healing Data Pipelines

Agents that detect, diagnose, and repair broken ETL jobs before dashboards go stale. Pre-seed. Seattle, US.

---

## The Problem

Data teams spend 40% of their week firefighting pipeline breakage. Schema
drift and upstream API changes break jobs silently; business teams find out
from wrong numbers in dashboards.

---

## The Product

DataForge watches lineage and job telemetry, localizes root cause on failure,
and proposes a tested patch as a pull request. Works with dbt, Airflow, and
Dagster.

---

## Traction

4 design partners (unpaid), 310 pipeline incidents auto-diagnosed in pilots.
Patch acceptance rate 64%.
