# BrainVC — The Operating System for Venture Capital

AI-first system that sources founders, verifies every claim, and decides a $100K check in under 24 hours. Pre-seed. Boston, US.

---

## The Problem

Venture capital runs on warm intros. Founders without networks stay invisible;
diligence takes weeks; by the time a fund sees a founder clearly, equally strong
ones have given up waiting. Capital flows through networks, not merit.

---

## The Product

BrainVC is a pipeline, not a chatbot: inbound applications and outbound scanning
converge into one funnel — claim extraction, three independent scoring axes, an
adversarial validator that cross-checks every claim against internal and web
evidence, and an evidence-backed memo where every number traces to a source.

---

## Traction

Working system built in under 24 hours at the 6th Hack-Nation Global AI Hackathon.
Live pipeline moves an application from deck to guardrailed investment decision
in under 2 minutes, with per-claim trust scores and external web verification.

---

## Team

---

## Technology & Defensibility

The moat is the memory layer: an append-only Founder Score that follows the
person across applications and never resets. Trust rules are code, not vibes —
contradicted revenue claims structurally cannot produce an invest decision.

---

## Market

VC deal-sourcing and diligence tooling is fragmented across CRMs and analysts.
A fund running BrainVC evaluates 100x more opportunities at the same headcount.

---
