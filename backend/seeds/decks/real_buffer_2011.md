# Buffer — A Smarter Way to Share on Social Media

Freemium SaaS for scheduling social media posts. Seed. San Francisco, US. (2011)

---

## Traction

55,000 registered users.
800 paying customers.
$150,000 annual revenue run rate.
1.5 million updates posted through Buffer.
97% gross margins.

---

## Milestones

Launched in early 2011.
October 2011: reached 55,000 users and the $150K revenue run rate.
December 2011: 50 application integrations.
Projection: 1 million users and $3.6M in revenue by January 2013.

---

## Problem

Sharing on social media at the right times is manual and inconsistent.
Posting everything at once floods followers; spacing posts out by hand
does not scale for professionals and businesses.

---

## Product

Buffer queues your posts and publishes them automatically at optimal
times across Twitter, Facebook, and LinkedIn. Add to your Buffer from
anywhere via browser extension and 50+ integrated apps.

---

## Business Model

Freemium subscription SaaS: free tier plus paid plans starting at
$10/month for individuals and teams.
