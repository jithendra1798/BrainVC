# ShelfSense — Computer Vision for Retail Shelves

Shelf cameras + AI that catch out-of-stocks the moment they happen. Pre-seed. Chicago, US.

---

## The Problem

Retailers lose 4% of revenue to out-of-stocks that staff notice hours late.
Manual shelf audits are sporadic and inaccurate.

---

## The Product

Low-cost battery cameras clip to shelf rails; on-device models flag gaps,
misplaced items, and price-tag mismatches. Alerts route to store staff phones.

---

## Traction

Deployed in 9 stores across 2 regional grocery chains. $12K MRR.
Detection accuracy 96% in production.
