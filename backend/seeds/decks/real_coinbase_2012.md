# Coinbase — The Easiest Way to Buy and Use Bitcoin

Hosted bitcoin wallet and payments network. Seed. San Francisco, US. (2012)

---

## What is Bitcoin

Bitcoin is a digital currency, three years old, enabling instant,
international, low-fee payments. Bitcoin transaction volume is about
$2 million per day.

---

## Problem

Existing bitcoin tools are far too difficult for regular people:
users must install software and manage their own cryptographic keys.
Bitcoin will not reach mainstream adoption this way.

---

## Product

Coinbase is a hosted bitcoin wallet: sign up like any web app, buy
bitcoin with a connected bank account, and send or receive payments on
web and mobile. A merchant API lets businesses accept bitcoin payments.

---

## Traction

Signups growing 20% per day.
$65,000 in transaction volume in the first 5 weeks after launch.
