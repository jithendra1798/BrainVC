# PromptPantry — Your AI Sous-Chef

Consumer app that turns whatever is in your fridge into dinner ideas. Pre-seed. Berlin, Germany.

---

## The Problem

Home cooks waste food and default to takeout because deciding what to cook
is exhausting. Recipe apps assume you already shopped.

---

## The Product

Snap a photo of your fridge; PromptPantry identifies ingredients and generates
step-by-step recipes with substitutions. Voice mode guides you while cooking.

---

## Traction

28K downloads in 4 months, 3.2K weekly actives. 4.6 App Store rating.
No revenue yet; premium tier launching next quarter at €4.99/month.
