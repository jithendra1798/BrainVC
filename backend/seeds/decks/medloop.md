# MedLoop AI — Prior Authorization on Autopilot

AI agents that draft and track insurance prior-authorization requests for clinics. Seed. Boston, US.

---

## The Problem

US clinics spend 16 hours per physician per week on prior authorizations.
Denial rates climb yearly; appeals are manual and inconsistent.

---

## The Product

MedLoop ingests the EHR record, drafts the PA request with payer-specific
evidence, tracks status, and auto-drafts appeals on denial. Human sign-off
on every submission.

---

## Traction

6 clinics live, 2,100 PAs processed. First-pass approval rate 87% vs. 71%
industry baseline. $9K MRR.
