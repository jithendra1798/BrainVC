# NimbusOps — Autonomous Cloud Cost Optimization

AI agents that continuously rightsize your cloud spend. Pre-seed. San Francisco, US.

---

## The Problem

Companies waste 32% of cloud spend on idle and oversized resources (Flexera 2025).
FinOps teams are reactive: dashboards flag waste weeks after the bill lands.
Engineers ignore cost tickets because fixing them risks breaking production.

---

## The Product

NimbusOps deploys read-only agents into your AWS/GCP account that:
1. Model each workload's real utilization curve.
2. Propose rightsizing changes as reviewable pull requests.
3. Auto-apply approved policies during safe windows.

Median customer saves 24% in the first 60 days.
